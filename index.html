Portuguese Recipes

Description

The Portuguese diet, even though it is restricted to a relatively small geographic space, shows Atlantic and also Mediterranean influences, as is visible in the amount of fish traditionally consumed.

Much has changed since Strabo referred to the Lusitanians as a people who fed on acorns.
